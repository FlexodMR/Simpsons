Product Goals

  • Make people laugh
  • Authentic Simpsons Experience
  • Simpsons style of humor and delivery

 

Story Goals

  • New stories that are authentic to characters’ relationship to experience
  • Incorporate “one off” or “red herring” missions that have little or nothing to do with the story
    • Mix of what characters “would do” and “have done”
  • Surprise the player
    • No predictability
  • Should be engaging regardless of Simpsons license (in other words, have a sound story structure including setups of humorous situations)
