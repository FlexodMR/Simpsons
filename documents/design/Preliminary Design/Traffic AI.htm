Traffic AI

 

Goals

 

  • Road network includes information needed for AI decisions
  • AI makes real-time decision about which way to go
    • Doesn’t have to obey traffic signs or traffic lights

 

Features

 

  • Road network includes information needed for AI decisions
    • # of lanes
    • Direction of lanes?
    • Distance to “side of road”
    • Intersections connect up to four roads
    • Traffic light control/object at intersections
    • Traffic density
    • Traffic type
    • Speed limit

 

Tools

 

  • Tuning variables that interpret the art road directives (e.g., define what “light traffic” means)

 

Content

 

  • Game world art includes “embedded” information about the road network; changing the road network automatically updates the AI paths
