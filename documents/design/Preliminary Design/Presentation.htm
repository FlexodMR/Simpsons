Goals

 

Major goals:

 

  • Choose a style for our game (e.g., the movie “Snatch”) and use it throughout
  • Utilize The Simpsons style as well
    • Camera cuts rather than camera swoops
      • Be careful to maintain continuity and context between cuts
    • Short scenes – camera changes every 5 seconds (or less – subject to research/analysis of Simpsons style)
  • Camera composition and purpose
    • Use the camera purposefully – no floaty, meaningless camera swoops
    • Show off the world – use conversations and other presentation elements to frame the bet world art

 

Minor goals:

 

  • Better tools – plan and coordinate roles of artists, designers, and coders
  • Don’t violate TRC requirements – specifically, everything can be paused
  • Allow movies to be skipped (possibly with a confirmation overlay, as in Jak & Daxter)
  • Fully specify HUD animation
  • Use sound to augment presentation – for example, stylized sound effects for slow motion and accelerated-time shots; see The Matrix, Snatch, and Max Payne as reference examples
  • Don’t use cuts on world interaction presentation – need to show relative location to the user; do use presentation style though – for example, the accelerated-time + slowed-time cuts used in Snatch
