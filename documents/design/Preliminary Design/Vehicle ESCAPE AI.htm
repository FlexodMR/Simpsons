Vehicle Escape AI

 

Goals

  • Promote re-use (i.e., higher priority for a system that can be used in different ways)
  • Reasonable scope
  • Fast and exciting gameplay – the model is the movie car chase (French Connection, Ronin, Bullet, etc.)
  • Limit predictability of AI

 

Prioritized Features

 

  • Disable an escaping vehicle
    • Vehicle on pre-defined path (but using AI to navigate obstacles in the world)
    • Choices in the vehicle path to reduce predictability (e.g., designers place the path, and specify where vehicle can make a choice about path)
    • Game variants that use the same tools/code:
      1. Tag (touch an escaping vehicle)
      2. Follow (don’t lose site – stay within a certain distance until you both reach a location)
    • AI vehicles give hints (e.g., turn signals – like the dumb criminals on “Cops” – we’d make a joke about it)
  • Tunable AI parameters:
    • Top speed  (could be used as simple race?)
    • Acceleration
    • Handling
    • Strength/durability
    • Reaction time/skill
  • AI car identified with an arrow, and possible other f/x or glow (same system as used in Multiplayer)
    • Include simple damage meter (see Wreckless for example)
  • Hunt and destroy – a number of AI cars that use ordinary traffic AI to drive around the city, you have to catch and disable them
    • Less predictable – cars make their own decisions
    • Less tuned and controlled, but fun and cheap enough to be a nice change of pace
  • AI vehicles escape strategies:
    • Swerve through oncoming traffic
    • Powerslides/tight turns
    • Knock physics objects/vehicles into your path
    • Very fast driving through busy streets
    • Use jumps
    • Use counter-measures? (smoke screens, oil slicks, etc) needs prototyping - could be frustrating

 

 

Feature ideas we aren’t doing:

  • Escort – too difficult on camera and game interface
  • Tail the vehicle
    • Requires separate HUD element
    • Requires AI variations (e.g., Ai pause points)
    • Too slow?
    • Not reusable?
