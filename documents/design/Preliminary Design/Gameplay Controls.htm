Gameplay Controls

 

Goals

 

  • Smooth driving controls that give you full control over the vehicle
  • On-foot controls as simple as possible

 

Features

 

  • Driving controls
    • Analog or d-pad steering
    • Analog gas and brake controls. Activated by any of the following:
      • Controller buttons (with analog support)
      • Right analog stick
      • Gas/brake pedals on steering wheel hardware
    • Counter-measure button
    • E-brake button (could experiment with analog input)
    • Taunt button
    • Horn button?
    • Experiment with controlling both steering and gas/brake on one joystick (ala Blood Wake)
  • On-foot controls:
    • Analog movement, with the d-pad or analog joystick
      • Characters walk and run through this one control (i.e., move the stick just a little for walking). Or do we still need walking, now that we don’t have stealth AI?
      • Characters should accelerate to full running speed, and decelerate to a stop. The deceleration should include a character-specific animation (e.g., Bart sliding to a stop like Mario 64).
      • Most importantly, the control and animation should feel smooth
    • Manual camera control with the right analog stick? Or does it auto-rotate? See camera document.
    • Do we need an “action button” to push “buttons” or interact with characters? Or is it totally automatic?
      • A button gives us the advantage of allowing on/off toggle switches (e.g., open a door, drive your car through, then close the door behind you?). Or would this be easier to do with two separate buttons? In other words, can ALL buttons have just one function? (e.g., open or close, start or stop)
    •  

 

Tools

 

  • On-foot:
    • Tune the sensitivity of the controller (walk/run states, dead zone, etc.)
    • Tune penalty for 180 degree turns
    • Tune character acceleration/deceleration
    • Tune run/walk speed
  • Vehicle:
    • Tune sensitivity of steering and gas/brake
    • Tune which buttons are used for gas/brake/e-brake/taunt
