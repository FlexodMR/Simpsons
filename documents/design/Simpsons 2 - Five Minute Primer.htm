Simpsons 2 Gameplay Primer                                March 11, 2002

 

It’s a Driving Game

 

  • The core of the gameplay is driving artificial intelligence (AI)
  • Evade other vehicles
    • Lifelike AI
    • Wide range of countermeasures:
      • Driving maneuvers
      • Oil Slicks, Bullet Time, etc.
    • Vehicle choice matters
  • Chase other vehicles
  • Destroy vehicles and objects
  • Realistic driving model
    • Easier to drive at first
    • Rewards skill and experience (e.g., “Rally mode” driving on dirt)
  • Game world designed for chase/evade gameplay (jumps, escapes, hide, etc.)

 

The Game is The Interface

 

  • One front end (FE) screen – launches Story Mode or Multiplayer
  • In-game conversations for mission objectives
  • Change vehicles in-game – you are the passenger
  • Dialog and relationships – 3 people in a car at once
  • Interactive world basic puzzle solving (e.g., find the switch to turn on the ferry)
  • Scripted events (e.g., train or large vehicle triggered by player location)
  • Simple running gameplay:
    • Mario 64 controls
      • Save time by avoiding 180 degree turns
    • Simple escape gameplay (e.g., run from a scripted vehicle path) while running around obstacles

 

Head to Head Gameplay

 

  • Capture The Flag (CTF) gameplay
    • Subset of Downtown level
    • “Flag” is an object that spawns in various locations
    • Base stays constant throughout a game session
    • Bullet Time drives the gameplay:
      • Keep players close together
      • Emphasize contact and player battles
      • Give the advantage to the chaser
